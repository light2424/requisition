Instructions

Included in this package are the requisitions that may be required to prepare for issuing letters patent.Section A is mandatory to issue a letters patent.

Answer the following questions to determine which additional Sections need to be completed.

Required Supporting Documents

  • Survey plan
  • Legal Description
  • Market Value/Admin Fee
  • Legal Name(s) of the patentees
  • Corporate Profile Report/Extra Provincial Licence as needed if sale is to a corporation
  • P.I.N Printout of adjacent private property or P.I.N of leasehold parcel if enlarging to sale and patient
  • A copy of legal approval is also required if sale is to Quit claim
