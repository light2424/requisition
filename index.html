Entering of Sale and Issuance of Letters Patent

Instructions

Included in this package are the requisitions that may be required to prepare for issuing letters patent.

Answering the questions below will unlock additional sections for you to complete.

  1. Is the applicant currently under another form of tenure that requires cancellation?
  2. Is the sale associated with an easement accross the parcel that's being sold ?
  3. Is the sale for a crown shore reserve or road allowance?
  4. Is it within a municipality?

Section A: Statutory Authority for Sale and Issuance of Letters Patent (required)

Required Supporting Documents

  • Survey plan
  • Legal Description
  • Market Value/Admin Fee
  • Legal Name(s) of the patentees
  • Corporate Profile Report/Extra Provincial Licence as needed if sale is to a corporation
  • P.I.N Printout of adjacent private property or P.I.N of leasehold parcel if enlarging to sale and patient
  • A copy of legal approval is also required if sale is to Quit claim
